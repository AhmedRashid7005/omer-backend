Import solutions

Importing from the KSA, USA and PRC

Is it difficult for you to travel to the KSA, USA or PRC, while you have concerns about the cost of accommodation, transportation, scam of merchants, and other problems, with the final bill greater than the purchases you make inside your homeland?
Service sign in

You do not need to travel to the KSA, the USA or the PRC to check that the product matches the description, to negotiate with the seller or to look for the best price and quality. Now we can order items of all kinds and in the quantity you need, while processing all documents to deliver the shipment to you, through the cheapest air, sea and land shipping couriers.

Our experience

We have professional representatives in the KSA, USA and PRC, and we have the phone numbers of wholesale suppliers, factories and e-shops, so we will provide you with the best quality items and the cheapest prices.

You just need to fill out the import form (you can add an unlimited number of items), and you will be provided with the latest offers for each item (up to 3 offers for a single item).


Note: If you have a link of the item you want to order, please refer to the (Brokerage service) page.

Targeted Audience

Customers from the KSA & Bangladesh

If you are from the KSA or Bangladesh, you can import from the USA and the PRC, even from Riyadh.

Other Customers

If you are from outside the KSA and Bangladesh, you can only import from the KSA.

How is the order shipped?

When you import from the USA and PRC, the shipment is delivered to your addresses in these countries, and then the shipment and documents are checked, packed and processed to ship it to your address in the KSA or Bangladesh


However, when you import from the KSA, the order will be delivered to the Saudi suite in the first place, and then the shipment and documents are checked, packed and processed to ship it to your worldwide address.

Service Fees


Relative commission

If the order value is more than 1000 SAR, a commission of 13% will be deducted from the total value of the order.

Fixed commission

If the order value is 1000 SAR or less, then a fixed commission of 150 SAR will be applied.

Fixed fees

car fuel + internal delivery fee + loading and unloading + packaging.

Import Guide

We review the order and send you an invoice containing the order details.

Fill out the import form, including all the required details, then submit the request.

Prepare a correct name, description and image of the item you want to order.

Order will be delivered to your suite. You can request extra services or have it shipped to your address worldwide.

We order the required items and provide you with updates and certified invoices.

Sign in to your account, and pay the costs and fees to confirm the order.
