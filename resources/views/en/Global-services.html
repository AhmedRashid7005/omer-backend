For worldwide customers


Worldwide customers can benefit from the website services and features, including:

The Saudi Suite

When local e-shops or merchants don't ship globally, a worldwide customer can benefit from the Saudi suite through using their own suite at our warehouse as a shipping address for their orders. Upon receiving the shipment on their behalf and recording it in their suite, they can demand to re-ship it to their doorstep anywhere in the world.

More

Website brokerage

A worldwide customer can order from local e-shops in the event of unavailability of Saudi local payment methods, of inability to deal with the e-shops or otherwise. Orders will be received at their Saudi suite in the first place, and then it will be re-shipped to their doorstep anywhere in the world.

Details

Import from Saudi Arabia

When a worldwide customer can't find certain items at local e-shops or wants to order a large quantity at a wholesale price, etc…, they can order it through the form available on our site, noting that we have well-trained representatives to get the best price and quality in the market. Not to mention that customers are provided with three offers available for each item accompanied with all details and costs of the order.

Details

Buyer and seller Protection service

When a customer wants to order or receive a personal item from a seller in the KSA, and the worldwide buyer does not have a payment method available, or fears that the seller would manipulate them, etc…, they can use this service, especially if the seller requests the payment before delivering the order. All you have to do is prompting the seller to use this service.
