What is the Saudi Suite?

It's your own address in the KSA, specifically in Riyadh. You can use it as a shipping address when ordering from local e-shops and sellers inside the KSA, since most local shops and sellers do not ship their products globally. However, when you use your own suite, your order will be delivered to our warehouse, and we will reship it to you anywhere worldwide.
