What is the American-Chinese suite?

It's your own address in our warehouses in the USA and PRC. Benefits include: 1. Get your orders shipped from US. shops to your US. address, 2. Get your orders shipped from Chinese shops to your address in the PRC, since most US. and Chinese shops do not ship to the KSA and Bangladesh, so the seller will treat you as if you are in the USA or the PRC. You can also benefit from the features and services of the address that you will not get in the case of direct shipping to your address in the KSA and Bangladesh.

An exclusive service for Saudi and Bangladeshi customers

Suite features & services

Product Image Library

You can get up to 25 HQ images of the item to view the details and content of the shipment clearly and to ensure that the shipment matches your orders.

Bulk orders

When you order from different e-shops or have bulk orders, you can save more than 70% on shipping, by combining shipments and shipping it in a single shipment.

Check your order

We can check the shipment content in your suite or the electronic devices especially if it is used, to ship it to you or return it to the seller.

Low-cost shipping

The cheapest global shipping cost ever from our warehouses to the KSA and Bangladesh (20 SAR/kg from the USA and 25 SAR/kg from the PRC).

Order Insurance

Insurance of 2% of the value of the shipment to remunerate you in full in the case of any defect, suspension or damage of the shipment by any party.

Shipment Safe Storing

All your shipments will be stored (up to 60 days of delivery) on separate shelves in a safe and secured warehouse equipped with the state-of-the-art techniques.

Product Video Library

You can get HD video that shows all the details of the order and review each item separately to ensure the packaging and content of the shipment and that the shipment matches your order.

Smart packaging

We will repack the shipments in high-quality boxes before they are reshipped to you, to ensure that there is no damage or breakage, because most of the incoming shipments are poorly packed.
