Buyer & Seller Protection service (locally and globally)

An exclusive online service that ensures the highest levels of protection for both the seller and the buyer, where the site works as a broker between the seller and the buyer to protect the transactions from fraud or manipulation by any party. This means that the order is shipped by the seller to the site, which shows all its details to the buyer online to complete the purchase, so as to ensure that the order is delivered to the buyer with the required specifications, and the seller gets their payment with no direct dealing with the buyer.

When the service could be used?

When ordering from an anonymous, unreliable, or unregistered seller.

When the seller and the buyer have no mutual trust or they are unable to deal together, directly.

When a party is unable to verify the personal information of the other party.

When the buyer needs a fast, secure and safe payment and delivery method.

When the buyer requires images and videos of the order to check it before shipping, or to return the order.

When the order delivery to the buyer and the payment to the seller are not guaranteed.

Service Subscription requirements:

Both parties must agree to the service terms and conditions.

Both the seller and the buyer must have registered accounts on the site.

The items ordered have to be tangible, legitimate and registered.

The seller has to be inside the KSA.

Service subscription fees:

For the seller

Delivery fees according to the courier and the size of the shipment to their suite at our warehouse in Riyadh, the KSA +a nominal amount upon buying the service (depending on the package)

For the buyer

A fixed fee when approving the service (25 SAR) + 2% commission of order value if it exceeds 500 SAR.

Request The Service

How to request the service:

The seller signs in to the site control panel and chooses the order then selects "Seller & Buyer Protection service".

The order will be recorded in the seller's suite and they will be notified of the same.

The seller shall ship their items to their suite on our warehouse in Riyadh .

Both the buyer and seller need to sign up to the site to get their Saudi suite .

The buyer can order shipment extra services or request to have it shipped to their doorstep upon the payment of the invoice.

The buyer signs in to the site control panel to approve the purchase of the order.

Our service team will approve the request, then the order details will be sent to the buyer's suite .

The seller fills in the required data along with the buyer's suite number in the service form .

The order is processed and shipped to the buyer's address anywhere in the world

The payment will be sent to the seller via their preferred payment method

The order is processed and shipped to the buyer's address anywhere in the world

The invoice includes the order value + delivery fees + other costs, if any .
