Global shipping from the KSA

We use the following shipping couriers: Aramex, FedEx, DHL, according to site's interest, the customer's location, and the type of the product, noting that the customer is totally free to choose from the couriers available on the site, provided that the site and the customer abide by the courier's regulations.

Shipping is made through the fastest couriers available within 9-12 working days (up to 40 SAR/kg). To accurately calculate the shipping fees, kindly use our fee calculator.

Local delivery inside the KSA

Orders are delivered inside Riyadh through Mrsool app for ease of use. While Zagel app, Saudi Post, or express shipping couriers are used for shipping to all regions of the KSA, noting that the customer is totally free to choose the courier and the speed of delivery.

US-Chinese shipping to the KSA

Unlike global costly shipping sites, we (Guaranteed Access website) take into account the conditions of customers and have affordable pricing that suits everyone regardless of the weight of the shipment (Pricing is for orders coming from the China and the USA only) .

  •  For a shipment of less than 250 grams = free-of-charge shipping depending on the package.
  •  For shipments of 250/grams-10/kg = 35 SAR per kg shipping fee.
  •  For shipments of 10/kg-30/kg = 30 SAR per kg shipping fee.
  •  For shipments of 30/kg-50/kg = 25 SAR per kg shipping fee.
  • To calculate other weights, please go to the calculator page.

Shipment Delivery Time

After requesting the shipment of your order, we will receive it at our warehouse in Riyadh within 3-5 days, and once it is received, it will be resent from our warehouse to your address in Riyadh within 24-48 hours. However, if your location is outside the city of Riyadh, this will take about 2-3 days, depending on the region.
