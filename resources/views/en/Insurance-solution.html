When insurance is applicable?

Insurance on orders from the KSA, USA and PRC.

When shipping orders from the USA and PRC to the KSA.

When shipment is delivered locally or shipped globally from KSA.

How important is order insurance?

1. When ordering products:

When ordering products from shops and markets in the KSA, USA and PRC, insurance for the order is 70% important (3% of order price).


When ordering products from global e-shops (brokerage), insurance for the order is 25% important (2% of order price).

2. When shipping products globally:

When globally shipping an order from the KSA, you need compulsory insurance (5% of shipment value) .

When shipping an order from the USA and PRC to the KSA, you are free to make insurance (3% of shipment value) .

3. When orders are delivered locally:

When an order is delivered locally from Riyadh to other Saudi regions, insurance for the order is 80% important (2% of order price).

How to calculate order total cost?

If the order value is 2000 SAR, the tax is 100 SAR, and the shipping fee is 250 SAR, then the total amount will be 2350 SAR. And since the cost of insurance is calculated from the value of orders only (5% of order value, to say), you will have to pay only 100 SAR.

When should you make insurance for an order?

If it's difficult to verify the credibility of a seller or a website.

If the order is perishable foods or medicaments that need special care.

If the order is too expensive, large, or fragile.

In the case of bulk and/or several separated shipments.

If there are concerns of a certain shipping courier or of order delivery.

Remuneration you get from order

Full remuneration

In the case that an order is insured, our site is responsible for it until it is delivered to the customer

Full remuneration is paid upon the loss of an order, whether during shipping by the seller, at the seller's territory, at the warehouse or during local delivery

Full remuneration is paid when the delivery of an order is delayed for more than 30 days, starting from the date of completion of an order by customer. However, mass and delayed orders because of the customer or because of force majeure are excluded .

Partial remuneration

When insurance is not made, we contact the seller, the courier or third parties, and file a claim, if necessary, trying to refund the amount only

How can you pay for the insurance?

Insurance is paid in advance for any order or shipment, a notification will pop up upon completion of the payment.

Insurance is subject to the terms and conditions of the site agreement, kindly read it carefully.
