Website features and services for customers inside the KSA

Order free-of-charge shipping

Orders will be shipped from the USA and PRC to your address for free, if the shipment weight is 300 gm or less and depending on your package.

Cash Payment

When ordering items, you can pay in cash and an authorized representative will come and invoice you within Riyadh only.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

You can pay order and shipping costs from the USA and the PRC when the shipment is delivered to your address. This service is available for all Saudi regions.

Other Features

Features and services for all customers (inside and outside the KSA)

Installment Payments

When ordering items, you can pay a deposit to prove your seriousness, then you need to pay the remaining amount later along with the shipping cost.

Order Value Full Remuneration

In the case of shipment track default, delay, etc…, you will be remunerated in full.

Technical support

Your account's private manager and professional support team are always ready to respond to all your inquiries and complaints and resolve them ASAP.

Real weight calculation

The real weight of all shipments to and from the KSA is calculated, except for some couriers if there is a big difference between the real and volumetric weights.

Low-cost global shipping

Our shipping fees from and to the KSA are the cheapest ever, where the cost per kilo may up to 20 SAR only, with the fastest delivery to your doorstep within 3-5 working days only.

Various shipping couriers

We have agreements with several global and local shipping couriers to serve our customers and meet their needs when shipping the orders, with a low-cost shipping fee and the fastest delivery.

Sea freight

We have agreements with sea freight couriers to get the lowest cost for large and bulk shipments, depending on the country of the recipient, and the quantity and the quality of the shipment.

Customs Clearance

We process all documents related to the shipment and the customer with the final bill of lading (BOL), and attach them to the shipment to ensure the non-occurrence of shipment-related issues or delays.

Bulk orders

We have fully-equipped facilities and a highly-qualified team for sorting and packing bulk shipments in boxes and containers along with all necessary documents.

No hidden fees

All costs and fees payable by you will be shown clearly and in detail for you to review it on our site, in the package details and upon paying the final invoice.

The calculator

An accurate integrated calculator for all services. You can review the global and local shipping fees, the insurance cost, the commissions, the services and the exchange rate.

Order Tracking

Upon ordering and shipping orders, you can check the latest updates related to it, including the update history, the location and the expected time of delivery.
