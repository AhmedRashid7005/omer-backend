Delivery of an order to the buyer with receipt of the amount

It is a service that enables the seller to get a very low shipping cost to deliver the order to the buyer and receive the sales amount from it.

It is recommended to use in the following cases:

a- When the buyer does not have the means to pay the purchase amount.

b- When the seller is concerned about the buyer receiving the order and not paying the amount.

c- When you can't go to the shipping companies to ship the order to the buyer.

Reviews:

Service charges

39 if sales are 500 riyals and less.

6% if sales are more than SR 500.
